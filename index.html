Dhikr & Remembrance

Engage in the beautiful practice of remembering Allah through authentic supplications and dhikr

Morning Supplications

Asbahna wa asbahal-mulku lillah
"We have reached the morning and at this very time the dominion belongs to Allah."
Recommended: 1 time

Evening Supplications

Amsayna wa amsal-mulku lillah
"We have reached the evening and at this very time the dominion belongs to Allah."
Recommended: 1 time

Tasbih (Glorification)

SubhanAllah (Glory to Allah) 33 times
Alhamdulillah (Praise to Allah) 33 times
Allahu Akbar (Allah is Greatest) 34 times

Istighfar (Seeking Forgiveness)

Astaghfirullaha al-azeem alladhi la ilaha illa huwa al-hayyu al-qayyumu wa atubu ilayh
"I seek forgiveness from Allah the Mighty, whom there is none worthy of worship except Him, the Living, the Eternal, and I repent to Him."
Recommended: 100 times daily

Salawat (Blessings on Prophet)

Allahumma salli ala Muhammad wa ala ali Muhammad
"O Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad and upon the family of Muhammad."
Especially on: Fridays

Comprehensive Dhikr

La ilaha illa Allah wahdahu la sharika lah, lahu al-mulku wa lahu al-hamdu wa huwa ala kulli shay'in qadir
